## When You Need a Commercial License

You need a commercial license if **any** of the following apply:

- You embed svg2ooxml in a proprietary product or service
- You modify svg2ooxml and don't want to publish your changes
- You offer svg2ooxml's functionality over a network (SaaS) without
  releasing your application's source code
- You distribute software that includes svg2ooxml to customers who
  don't receive the full corresponding source

The AGPL requires that all users of your application — including users
who interact with it over a network — receive access to the complete
source code of the combined work. A commercial license removes this
requirement.

## FAQ

**Can I evaluate before purchasing?**
Yes. The AGPL version is fully functional. Use it for evaluation, and
purchase a commercial license before shipping to production.

**Can I use it for internal tools?**
If the tool is only used by your employees and not offered as a service,
you may not need a commercial license under AGPL. Contact us if unsure.

**What if I'm a solo developer or non-profit?**
We offer discounted or free commercial licenses for individual developers,
non-profits, and educational institutions. Reach out.

**Do I need a license for the Figma plugin?**
The hosted Figma plugin is a separate service with its own subscription.
The commercial license is for embedding the library in your own software.
