Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2, 2025

1. Introduction

svg2ooxml ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our SVG to Google Slides conversion service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use svg2ooxml, we collect the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture (provided by Google OAuth)
  • Authentication Tokens: Temporary OAuth tokens to access your Google Drive and Slides on your behalf
  • SVG Content: The SVG files you choose to convert
  • Usage Data: Service usage statistics, error logs, and performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity using Firebase Authentication
  • Create and export presentations to your Google Slides account
  • Convert SVG files to PPTX format
  • Monitor and improve service performance
  • Debug errors and provide technical support

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is our top priority:

  • No Permanent Storage: SVG files and converted presentations are processed in memory and immediately discarded
  • Encrypted Tokens: OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet) and stored temporarily in Google Cloud Secret Manager
  • Automatic Deletion: Authentication tokens are automatically deleted after each conversion
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security

5. Google API Services

svg2ooxml's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We request the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • drive.file: To create and manage presentations you create with our service
  • presentations: To write slide content to your Google Slides

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google: For authentication and Google Slides API access
  • Google Cloud Platform: For hosting and infrastructure services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Data Retention

  • OAuth Tokens: Deleted immediately after conversion
  • SVG Files: Never stored, processed in memory only
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 30 days for debugging purposes
  • Authentication Records: Minimal session data retained for security monitoring

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Access information about your data usage
  • File a complaint with data protection authorities

9. Children's Privacy

svg2ooxml is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
