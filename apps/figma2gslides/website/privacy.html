Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

1. Introduction

figma2gslides ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the hosted figma2gslides workflows and related service integrations built on svg2ooxml.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on which hosted features you use, we may process:

  • Account Information: Basic profile information from the identity provider used for hosted sign-in
  • Authentication Tokens: Temporary access tokens required for enabled third-party export integrations
  • Conversion Inputs: SVG files and related payloads you choose to convert
  • Generated Outputs: Presentation files or export artifacts produced during the workflow
  • Operational Data: Service logs, error traces, and usage metrics needed to operate and debug the service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate access to hosted workflows when sign-in is required
  • Perform requested conversions and deliver the resulting output
  • Execute third-party export integrations that you explicitly authorize
  • Operate, secure, and improve the service
  • Investigate errors and provide technical support

4. Data Storage and Security

We aim to minimize retained data and limit access to what is required to run the service:

  • Transient Processing: Conversion inputs and outputs are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to complete the workflow and operate the service
  • Scoped Tokens: Third-party access tokens are handled only for the integrations you enable
  • Operational Safeguards: Access controls, hosted-platform protections, and routine monitoring are used to limit unauthorized access
  • Local Use: Self-hosted or local package usage may avoid hosted processing altogether

5. Google API Services

If you use a hosted Google export workflow, figma2gslides' use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Those workflows may request scopes such as:

  • drive.file: To create and manage files generated through the authorized workflow
  • presentations: To write slide content when Google Slides export is enabled

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Identity and API Providers: To authenticate users and execute authorized export actions
  • Hosting and Infrastructure Providers: To run the website and service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or when necessary to protect rights, safety, or service integrity

7. Data Retention

  • Tokens: Retained only as long as required to complete the authorized workflow or maintain a valid session
  • Conversion Data: Retained only as needed to operate the service and resolve failures
  • Operational Logs: Retained for a limited period for monitoring, debugging, and abuse prevention
  • Support Records: Retained as needed to resolve user-reported issues

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to a connected Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Access information about your data usage
  • File a complaint with data protection authorities

9. Children's Privacy

figma2gslides is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us
